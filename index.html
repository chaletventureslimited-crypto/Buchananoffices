Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Can't find your answer below? Just get in touch — we reply the same day.

  • 2-month minimum lease
  • Startup perks available
  • 1. What's included in the monthly rent?

    Your monthly rent includes exclusive use of your private office, shared kitchen and toilet facilities, use of the Skye conference room, and your business postal address. No hidden charges.

  • 2. What is the minimum lease term?

    2 months minimum. After that, rolling monthly contracts — no long-term lock-in.

  • 3. Is there a deposit required?

    Yes, a refundable deposit equivalent to one month's rent, returned in full at the end of your agreement.

  • 4. Can I use the address for my business?

    Yes. Use the Buchanan Street address for your registered company and correspondence. Virtual address also available standalone.

  • 5. Do you offer startup discounts?

    Yes — rent-free periods and reduced rates for eligible early-stage businesses. Get in touch and tell us about your company.

  • 6. How do I arrange a viewing?

    Get in touch via the contact form or email. Viewings are relaxed and informal — no sales pressure.
